# CleanerZX Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 15, 2026

CleanerZX helps users scan and manage photos, videos, contacts, and device storage.

## Information We Access

CleanerZX may request access to:

- Photos and videos, so you can review and clean large files, duplicates, and clutter
- Contacts, so you can find duplicate, empty, or incomplete contact cards
- Location, only to center the Places map when you choose to use that feature
- Notifications, so the app can send cleanup reminders if you enable them

## How We Use Information

CleanerZX uses this information only to provide app features on your device.

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not use your information for third-party advertising.

## Data Storage

Most processing happens on your device. CleanerZX may store app preferences, scan history, cleanup progress, and subscription status locally on your device.

## Subscriptions

CleanerZX Pro subscriptions are managed by Apple through the App Store. Payment and billing information is handled by Apple.
